SDR · ICP

Who to call and what to listen for

Good fit

  • •Running Datadog at scale (500+ hosts or heavy custom metrics)
  • •Platform / SRE / observability team owns the stack
  • •Adopting or standardizing on OpenTelemetry
  • •Complaints about agent redeploys or pipeline toil

Trigger phrases

  • "Our Datadog renewal is coming up…"
  • "Finance is asking about the metrics line item…"
  • "Every pipeline change means redeploying agents…"
  • "We tried Elastic before — UX was rough…"
Last trigger = perfect opener: "We've rebuilt the front door — Serverless + Streams. Worth 30 minutes?"
SDR · Discovery

Five questions before you book

1. Spend

"Roughly what are you paying Datadog today — and is custom metrics a big chunk?"

2. Scale

"How many hosts, services, or custom metric names are you tracking?"

3. Pain

"What's driving the conversation — cost, OTel migration, or ops toil?"

4. Timing

"When's your Datadog renewal or budget review?"

5. Stakeholders

"Who owns observability — and who signs off on platform spend?"

Pass / no-go

No Datadog, no metric volume, no pain, no timeline → nurture, don't book.

AE · Demo

30-minute flow — don't skip step 2

1
Elastic Streams (5 min)
Live retention + downsampling. Server-side vs. agent-side. This is the hook.
2
TCO calculator (5 min)
o11y-compare.vercel.app + their volumes from SDR notes. Show the number. Stop talking.
3
Managed OTLP ingest (5 min)
One endpoint, one key. Contrast with Datadog agent setup complexity.
4
Agent Builder triage (5 min)
Alert → logs → summary. "Can Datadog do this without three extra SKUs?"
5
Next step (5 min)
Propose Serverless POC. Confirm renewal timeline and economic buyer.
AE · Pushback

When they push back — be honest, then redirect

"We already use Datadog Pipelines."

Pipelines are client-side — every rule change means redeploy. Streams is server-side: change retention from the UI, zero agent work.

"Elastic UX isn't as good as Datadog."

Agree — older deployments had friction. Show Serverless. Time the same workflow: alert → root cause. Most haven't seen the new experience.

"Datadog supports OTel too."

They route through their agent and schema. Elastic stores what you send — open formats, no lock-in.

Gaps to acknowledge: OOB dashboards (Prom), infra map. Redirect to Streams + cost + Serverless POC.
